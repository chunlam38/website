A little bit about me 💁🏻‍♂️

Hello! I'm Chun (he/him). I'm a student-athlete at Brown majoring in Computer Science.

I was born & raised in Hong Kong (shoutout 852 friends) and hadn't lived anywhere else until I moved to Brown University in Rhode Island.

Nevertheless, thanks to tennis, I've had the chance to travel to countries I never thought I'd ever see like Fiji, Mauritius, and Budapest. By far though, Hong Kong is still the best city (not biased).

Prior to Brown, I had never coded but that quickly changed after taking CS0111 (one of Brown's intro courses). I really enjoyed the problem-solving aspect of it and haven't looked back since.

I’m a big Chelsea fan, recently got addicted to caffeine, and have an insatiable appetite 🫃🏻.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, add me on Insta, or stalk my tennis career on here. You can also email me.
